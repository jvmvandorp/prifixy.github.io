Optix

Optix was made with little scripts to help people gain more control over their Windows settings, focussed on privacy. Then I made a few scripts for debloating and saw that my general performance was way better than before. That was the moment I started developing programs to boost FPS. When I started I used mainly Batch and PowerShell, but now I work with more advanced languages, to keep Optix as clean as possible.
